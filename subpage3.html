POSTER DESIGN ASSIGNEMENT

This assignment is an extension of ASSIGNMENT 2. With our logo in mind we had to create a poster that shared some kind of information to whoever was viewing it.

I decided to make the poster my brand's announcement of the rebrand from the logo assignment. Using some themes like gradients, tearing, textures I tried to make the poster look like it had been wheat-pasted onto a wall. I also included some information about my design decisions when working on the logo assignment.
