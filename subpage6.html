ZINE DESIGN

For this assignment, we first had to create a 4 page zine to loosen up our IndDesign skills. Having never been taught InDesign, I was excited to learn from a teacher instead of googling my problems.

Next we had to create at least a 16 page zine for the final. I decided to focus on my thesis work for the topic of this zine. Narrowing down all the work I have created over the past semester to fit onto 16 pages was extremely challenging. I decided to expand the zine to 20 pages (as long as its in multiples of four it could be any number greater than 16 pages). Still I found it hard to cull a lot of my work/information.
